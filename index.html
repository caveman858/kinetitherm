About kinetitherm

At kinetitherm, we believe the future of power lies in harnessing the fundamental forces around us. Our mission is to lead the charge in energy innovation by developing cutting-edge technologies that efficiently convert and store kinetic and thermal energy. We are dedicated to creating a sustainable tomorrow, driving breakthroughs that provide reliable, clean, and accessible energy for all.

Our expert team of scientists and engineers is committed to rigorous research and development, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in energy efficiency and environmental stewardship.

Our Innovative Technologies

Advanced Kinetic Converters

Developing highly efficient systems that capture mechanical motion and convert it into usable electrical or thermal energy, from micro-scale vibrations to large-scale industrial processes.

High-Density Thermal Storage

Creating materials and systems capable of storing significant amounts of thermal energy for extended periods, enabling consistent power supply and waste heat recovery.

Synergistic K-T Integration

Developing hybrid solutions that seamlessly integrate kinetic and thermal principles for optimal energy harvesting, conversion, and long-duration storage across various applications.
